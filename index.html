Have Any Questions?

What is cryptocurrency?
A digital currency that using cryptography technologies which was initiated by satoshi Nakamoto.
What is ICO?
Stands for initial coin offering, a crowdfunding which offering utility and launched on centralized platform
What is IDO?
Stands for initial dex offering, also a crowdfunding which offering utility and launched on decentralized platform.
IDO vs ICO?
The key differences are, ICO launched on centralized exchange, whereas IDO is on decentralized, IDO is based on liquidity Pool whereas ICO id based on bid & ask. Centralized exchange is more globalized and regulated compare to decentralized.
How far is the crypto potential?
Crypto is evolving from day by day, the total market capitalization (market cap) for crypto is more than 2 trillion USD as reported by coinmarketcap.com on 7/9/2021. Many countries have accepted cryptocurrency slowly including the latest, where El Salvador announced they are accepting bitcoin as legalized tender which reported by CNBC.com .
